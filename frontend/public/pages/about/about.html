About Us

We are a small team of privacy advocates and developers who believe that online freedom
is a fundamental right - not a privilege.

Blueberry VPN was built out of frustration. We were tired of big corporations tracking every
click, every search, every move. So we decided to build something better.

Our mission is simple: give people back control over their digital lives. No logs, no tracking,
no selling your data. Just a fast, secure, and honest VPN service.

We are not backed by investors who want your data. We are backed by users who value their privacy.

Why Blueberry? Because we're small, we're independent, and we actually care.
Every feature we build, every server we add — it's for you, not for advertisers.
