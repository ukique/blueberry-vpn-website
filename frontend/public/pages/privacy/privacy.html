Why privacy is important?

In the digital age, your personal data is one of the most valuable things you own.
Every website you visit, every message you send,
every purchase you make — it all leaves a trace.

Without privacy, corporations and governments can track your behavior,
build profiles about you, and use that data in ways you never agreed to.
Your location, habits, and preferences become a product sold to the highest bidder.

Privacy is not about hiding. It's about control — the right to decide who sees your data and when.
A VPN encrypts your connection, hides your IP address, and gives that control back to you.
